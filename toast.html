Toast Recipe

Description

So here we can see a lasagna which origin is found in the land of italy. But now there comes the exciting part: The recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Chees
  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Buy ingredients
  2. Put the ingredients organized on a table
  3. Make the dough by mixing the water with flour and some salt
  4. Put the dough under a blanket and wait 30min
  5. Take the dough and cut it like sandwhich
  6. Put the dough in a pot and mix it with your wished spices
  7. Then put everything in a big toaster
  8. When done enjoy!
